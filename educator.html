The Thread That Runs Through Everything

Whether I was crouching beside a desert tortoise in Nevada, speaking in front of a crowd, or writing for a developer audience, the goal stayed the same: help someone understand something they did not before, and care about it.

I design education around the learner first. That means meeting people where they are, adapting the format, and finding the moment of curiosity that makes information stick.

Conservation taught me to translate science for the public. Developer education taught me to translate complexity for technical communities.
